About Bhavya Play School

At Bhavya Play School, we believe in providing a nurturing and engaging environment for young children. As a branch of My Chota School, we follow a holistic approach to early childhood education that focuses on the overall development of the child.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster holistic development in children by creating an environment that nurtures their physical, emotional, intellectual, and social growth. We aim to cultivate a love for learning in every child, laying a strong foundation for their future education.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leader in early childhood education, recognized for our commitment to excellence and innovation. We aspire to shape confident, independent, and compassionate individuals who are prepared to succeed in a rapidly changing world.

Explore Our Programs

At Bhavya Play School, we offer a variety of programs designed to cater to the different needs and developmental stages of young children. Each program is crafted to promote holistic growth through fun, interactive, and educational activities.

Playgroup

Our Playgroup program is designed for children aged 2 to 3 years. We focus on socialization, motor skills, and early language development through engaging activities.

Nursery

Our Nursery program caters to children aged 3 to 4 years. It focuses on building foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and creative arts through play-based learning.

Kindergarten

Our Kindergarten program is for children aged 4 to 6 years. It prepares children for formal schooling by focusing on comprehensive development across cognitive, emotional, and social areas.
