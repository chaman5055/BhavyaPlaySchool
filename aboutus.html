About Us

Bhavya Play School is a branch of My Chota School, dedicated to nurturing young minds with love and care. We believe that the early years are the foundation of a child's educational journey, and we strive to provide a safe, joyful, and stimulating environment that encourages learning through play and exploration.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster holistic development in children by creating an environment that nurtures their physical, emotional, intellectual, and social growth. We aim to cultivate a love for learning in every child, laying a strong foundation for their future education.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leader in early childhood education, recognized for our commitment to excellence and innovation. We aspire to shape confident, independent, and compassionate individuals who are prepared to succeed in a rapidly changing world.

Our Core Values

Love & Care

We provide a nurturing environment where children feel loved, valued, and safe.

Creativity

We encourage creative expression and exploration through diverse activities and play.

Respect

We foster an environment of respect for others, the environment, and oneself.
